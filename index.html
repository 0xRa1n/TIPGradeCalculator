Merge Components Help

If your course has multiple components that are merged into one grade (e.g. Lab, Seatwork, Project all merged into one Assessment grade), you can use this helper to calculate the combined percentage for that grade.

Note that the component weight is the percentage contribution, and the component percentage is the overrall percentage you got for that component. For example, if your Lab grade contributes 35% to your overall Assessment grade, and you got an 90% for Lab, you would put 35 for the weight and 90 for the percentage.
